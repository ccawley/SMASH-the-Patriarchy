SMASH! the Patriarchy

Instructions

When you are ready to smash some patriarchs go ahead and click the Start the SMASH! button. Once the game starts pictures will start to show up randomly on the grid. Clicking on pictures of men will get you a point but be careful, not all the pictures are of men who've abused their power! You should be able to tell what you want to smash and what you shouldn't. If you happen to smash a puppy, for example, you'll lose two points so SMASH! carefully.

Also, consider challenging a friend. Loser donates a predetermined amount to one of the charities I've suggested or one of your choosing! Better yet, both donate and please continue to think about how you can use your place in society, regardless of you gender, to boost the voices of the women coming forward with their experiences of sexual harassment and continue to make society a little more equal and friendly for the women around you.

*Pro Tip!* Multiple SMASH!'s on the same bad dude just may let you rack up the points that much faster...
